Welcome to online bird voice trainer

This is a web app to train on bird voices.
You may select any birds and their voices and create a specific selection.

Be aware: the data is stored in the browser! That's why no login is needed.
But if you use a different browser or device, you have to select new data.

You may also export your data and import it into an other browser (in section "Trainings")

Start with Import species (click on the question mark near the search field for help)
