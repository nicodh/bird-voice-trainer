Welcome to online bird voice trainer

  1. Start by importing some species. Enter at least 3 characters of the birds name you are looking for
  2. When you selected a bird, some recordings are loaded from www.xeno-canto.org
  3. You can listen to the voices and check the ones you want to use in your training
  4. If you want you can add an image from Wikipedia of the bird, by clicking on the icon near the search field
  5. Save the data and select more birds*
  6. After having several species you can add a training by clicking on Trainings
  7. Enter a name for the new training and select the birds you want to train on
  8. After saving the new training you can start by clicking on "Trainer"
  9. The birds of the training will appear in random order and their calls will be also played in random order
  10. Type at least 3 charcaters of your guess and select the name of the bird
  11. See then result and carry on by clicking next

*Be aware: the data is stored in the browser! That's why no login is needed.
But if you use a different browser or your phone, you have to select new data.
You may also export your data and import it into an other browser (in section "Trainings")
