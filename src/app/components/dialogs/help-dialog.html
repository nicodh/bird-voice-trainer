Usage

Please enter a name for a species in your language. Wait for suggestions and click on the appropriate species.

Then wait for the recordings to load and select one or more recordings and save the selection.

Once you have several species added you might create a training and add the species you want to train on.

The training will be available then in the Trainer

Be aware that there is no account, so all your data is stored in your browser. If you change the browser or delete all caches your data is lost.

However you might create a backup (on page "Trainings") and restore it later or in other browsers.
